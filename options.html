FAQ

  • Why there is nothing changed after preferences updated?
  • After preferences are changed, please click the save button and refresh the Gmail page.
  • Are those changes permanent?
  • The changes are always local. In worst case, reinstall or remove the extension should get everything restored.
  • How do I minimize the left sidebar instead of hiding it?
  • Just click the burger menu () at top left of screen, it's a built-in feature of Gmail.
  • How do I hide the widget bar at right hand side?
  • Click the right arrow () at bottom right of screen, it's also a Gmail built-in feature.

  • Please submit your bug or suggestion here.
